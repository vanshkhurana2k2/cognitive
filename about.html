Our Mission

Putting your health in your hands

Cognitive seeks to radically transform healthcare by returning patient care back to the hands of the patient. Our goals as a company are to promote self-care through our app, the first neurocognitive diagnosis tool available on mobile devices. We believe that our commitment to self-care will help in the early diagnosis of millions of patients with cognitive impairment in the future.

Our Story

The Fight Against Neurodegenerative Disease

One in ten people over the age of 65 are affected

That comes out to 44 million people in the world who are afflicted. By 2050, healthcare costs for dementia in the US alone could rise to $1.1 trillion, over 325% more than what they are now. In a future where the average lifespan increases, risks for neurodegenerative disease will only go up.

Early detection of Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, Huntington’s, and other neurodegenerative diseases may greatly assist in patient care. At Cognitive, we aim to use the results of our Neurocognitive Screening test to better evaluate cognitive function through simple tasks representative of cognitive health.

Disclaimer

The Cognitive Neurocognitive Screening Diagnosis Tool is a patient supplement that allows patients to evaluate their cognitive abilities through simple tasks. While the test provides a metric to evaluate cognitive function, it cannot determine whether a patient has a disease like Alzheimer’s, for example. The test is not liable for any false negatives/positives that are presented through results.
